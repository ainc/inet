Penny for your thoughts? How about a stack of cash for your best idea?

iNet and Awesome Inc are teaming up to create the largest student-only business pitch competition ever at the University of Kentucky. Participation is ridiculously easy. Use your computer or smartphone to record your pitch, upload it to Youtube, share the link with us via the pitch application on this site, and you could win a chunk of the $1000 available in prize money!

Pitch Your Idea

Rules

  1. Pitches should be no more than 150 seconds (2:30 minutes). We will stop watching/caring at the 2:30 minute mark, so keep it simple and to the point.
  2. Pitches must be given by a current student enrolled at the University of Kentucky. Both full time and part time students are eligible.
  3. Group pitches are allowed as long as all participants are students enrolled at UK.
  4. See the Help page for Pitch Tips.
