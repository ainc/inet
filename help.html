Pitch Tips

  • Pitches must be less than 150 seconds (yes, we are so serious about this that we said it again)
  • Make sure you tell us what type of business you are starting. Example: "My idea is for a tandem bike service that transports students around UK."
  • If you have an analogy that makes understanding your idea simple, make sure to include it. Example: "My idea is to create Tinder for people over 85 years of age."
  • Make sure you tell us how your idea will make money. Example: "Our software product will secretly round off the decimals of bank transactions and deposit them into an offshore account."(Please don't do this: it's just a movie reference.)
  • If you have an awesome name for your product or service, please, please share it. Example: Snuggie (if they called it “Blanket with Sleeves”, this amazing product may have never made Scott Boilen a millionaire many times over.)
